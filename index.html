About US

About US

We are a group of students that is looking to work with art students to supply commissioned art for local businesses. We have businesses interested in working with us already and are looking for art students to produce the pieces in return for monetary compensation and brand exposure. Our ultimate goal is to work with the artists to help them develop and gain experience producing practical commissioned art while supplying the local area with custom, unique pieces for their businesses.
