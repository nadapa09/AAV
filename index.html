About Us

About Us

We are a dedicated group of individuals looking to create a bridge between the vast number of businesses in need of creative services and the sea of hungry art and design students looking for their first jobs. We handle all the icky stuff- the communication between artist and business, and the contracting. Our ultimate goal is to help as many student artists as we can to get started in their careers by building a unique and targeted body of work that speaks to the efficacy and quality of their work.

For the client: We provide the highest quality student art possible at a competitive price. To provide you with the highest quality work possible, we pair your job with a student artist who understands your business’s unique style and flair from our diverse portfolio of artists. Your commissioned project will be completed professionally and pain-free - we speak with the artist and direct the project, all you have to do is tell us what you want!

For the artists: We provide a prestigious and powerful vantage point from which to create. We get to know you and your work. Once we know who you are and what you’re capable of we assign curated tasks to you that will fit your qualifications specifically. This is different from a design collective in that we are doing the work of going out in the field and finding the jobs- so you get more time to focus more on what you’re good at! With our services you get exposure, payment, and experience.

Meet The Artists


Victoria

I am a painter, illustrator, and graphic designer currently pursuing a BA at the University of Michigan Penny Stamps School of Art and Design with a minor in Art History. When I’m not in my studio painting, you can find me exploring museums, discovering new artists on Instagram, hanging out with my friends and family, or doodling on any piece of paper I can get my hands on.


https://www.victoriabornstein.com


Courtney

Courtney Ignace is a illustrator and painter native to northern Michigan. Courtney’s work aims to create dialogue on social spaces, food, sustainability, and humor. Her favorite mediums are oil paint, ink, and watercolor. She is currently studying for her BFA in art and design and the University of Michigan.


https://courtneyignace.portfoliobox.net/


Ophelia

Artist, painter, traveler. I am a current Junior at the University of Michigan pursuing a BFA in Art and Design and a BA in International Studies. I was born and raised in the Greater Boston Area, and I have been oil painting and drawing for over ten years. Along the way I have picked up a mixture of other skills, and I am always on the look-out for a good conversation. My work is scattered with explorations of different mediums and techniques along with the human figure. I am constantly fascinated by parallel relationships between the edges in a painting, the figure and ground, and between a story and its audience.


https://odeng5.wixsite.com/odeng
